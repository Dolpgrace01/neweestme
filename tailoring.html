The Anatomy
of an Olb Suit

Every garment is a marriage of structural integrity and fluid aesthetics. We utilize canvas construction rather than fusing to ensure the garment breathes and molds to your body over time.

  • Internal Structure

    Full Horsehair Canvas

  • Lapel Finishing

    Hand-Rolled & Padded

  • Buttonholes

    Milanese Silk Stitch

  • Shoulder Expression

    Spalla Camicia (Neapolitan Style)
